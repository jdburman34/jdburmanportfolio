About Me

Passionate Agricultural Design Engineer with B.Tech in Agricultural Engineering from Uttar Banga Krishi Vishwavidyalaya (CGPA: 8.25) and specialized expertise in mechanical design, simulation, and agricultural machinery development.

Currently working at India's premier mechanical engineering research institute, CSIR-CMERI, where I combine traditional mechanical engineering principles with modern CAD and simulation technologies to solve real-world agricultural challenges. My expertise spans across Autodesk Inventor, ANSYS Workbench, MSC Adams, and advanced simulation tools for comprehensive product development and performance optimization.

Mission

To innovate and design cutting-edge agricultural machinery that enhances field efficiency, promotes sustainable farming practices, and empowers farmers with reliable, technology-driven solutions. My interdisciplinary approach combines mechanical design principles with precision agriculture technology, contributing to the future of automated harvesting solutions.
